        About me and
what I can do for you

I'm a student of software engineering with primary interests in machine learning, distributed systems, and brain-machine interfacing. My passions for creating useful solutions from code and contributing to a world where everyone's needs can be meet have lead me here. In addition to my professional interests, I also love hiking, sailing, theatre, dogs, basketball, listening to and making music, math, science (especially physics), video gaming, and philosophizing.

Take a look at my portfolio to see some examples of both my previous work and the side projects I've enjoyed. For clients with simpler web development needs, I offer several fixed-price packages. These are most apt for those of you who require construction of a content-based or a standard e-commerce site. Clients that have more specific development needs or custom web solutions are recommended to complete the client questionnaire. The questionnaire is an informal starting point for our client-contractor relationship. It helps me to both understand your needs and improve my services, as well as provide you with the means to communicate to me your initial plans/concerns for your project. A custom price quote estimate will be generated using the information gathered from the questionnaire, but more accurate pricing can only be determined after discussing your project with you personally.
